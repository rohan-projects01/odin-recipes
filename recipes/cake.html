Cake

Description

Fluffy vanilla cake made by creaming butter and sugar, then folding in eggs, flour, and a splash of milk and vanilla. Bake until golden, then cool and slather with a silky buttercream frosting.

Ingredients

  • sugar
  • flour
  • cocoa powder
  • leaveners
  • salt
  • eggs
  • milk
  • oil
  • vanilla
  • boiling water
